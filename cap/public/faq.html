FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS




Who can Donate Blood?

Any Donor, Who is healthy, Fit and not suffering from any transmittable diseases can donate blood.


How frequently can I donate blood?

A donar can again donate blood after 3 months of his/her last donation of blood.


Advantage of blood donation for a donor?

New blood cells will be generated after donation.


At what age can I donate blood?

Donor must be 18-60 years age and having a minimum weight of 50kg can donate blood.


What are the health benefits of donating blood?

Benefits of donating blood include good health and reduced risk of cancer and hemochromatosis. It helps in reducing the risk of damage to liver and pancreas. Donating blood may help in improving cardiovascular health and reducing obesity.
