FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS




What is the purpose of Psyberguide?
The main purpose of Psyberguide is to control ones's self, manage anger & stress and spread positivity.
How do I use it?
Using Psyberguide is easy. Get started by selecting one of the strategies. You'll be redirected to a customized daily plan with detailed instructions on how to overcome your weakness.


Do I need to pay for it?
Psyberguide is, and always will be, completely free! We're helping make the world a better, happier place.
What to do if I cannot overcome my weakness even after completing those 21 tasks daily?
You can take an appointment and consult a psychatrist, which is a benefit provided by Psyberguide.
Each strategy contains how many tasks?
Psyberguide consists of four strategies i.e, Self Control,Anger Management, Stress Relief & Positivity each comprises of 21 tasks to be competed within 21 days.
